ChargeVibe — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Overview

ChargeVibe ("we", "our", "the app") is a charging animation app for Android. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

ChargeVibe collects minimal data necessary for app functionality:

  • Battery information: Battery level, charging status, temperature, and voltage — used locally to display animations and dashboard. This data is never transmitted off your device.
  • App preferences: Your selected animation, speed settings, and unlock status — stored locally on your device using Android DataStore.
  • Advertising data: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns (screens visited, feature usage). This data is aggregated and anonymized.
  • Crash reports: Firebase Crashlytics collects crash data to help us fix bugs. This includes device model, OS version, and stack traces.

How We Use Information

  • Display charging animations based on battery state
  • Remember your preferences and unlocked content
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Improve app stability and performance

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Ad serving — Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics: Usage analytics — Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting — Privacy Policy

Data Storage

All app preferences and battery data are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain servers that store your personal information. Third-party services (AdMob, Firebase) may store data on their servers as described in their respective privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

ChargeVibe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You can reset your advertising identifier in your device settings. You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
